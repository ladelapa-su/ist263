For Night

Nighttime outfits tend to be a little “riskier” than daytime outfits—think darker colors, fabrics like leather and velvet, and high heels. I don’t wear anything too extreme, but there is definitely a hard line between what I would wear for a 1pm lunch vs. an 8pm dinner. These outfits are what I would wear to go to a late dinner, a bar (well, once I can get in at least!), a lounge, a party, or other nighttime occasions.
